About Me

My name is Thomas Stasko. I am 21 years old from Fairfield Connecticut and my favorite type of cuisine is Italian. I have a younger brother named Luke. My hobbies include, fishing, skiing, cooking, golf, and baseball. I also am very knowlegable of diesel engines. A fun fact about me is that I am a citizen of France as well as the United States!

I came to Babson College because of its reputation as a strong business school and diverse student body. Babson also provided me the opportunity to continue my atheltic career, although I no longer play on a varsity team. After graduation I plan to work for an outdoors company and put my marketing skills to work, while continuing to fish as much as possible. I hope to own my own boat one day and start a charter/commercial fishing business.

My Hobbies

My first hobby is fishing. I taught myself how to fish when I was 13 years old. Growing up in a coastal town in the northeast, my favorite fish to catch was striped bass because of their beauty and their ability to adapt to many different marine environments. I make my own flies and wooden lures to catch them. I also love to target bluefin and yellowfin tuna. The largest tuna I have ever caught was over 100 inches long and weighed over 300 pounds. Although I still fish in the winter, I spend more time skiing than fishing during this time. I have been skiing for 15 years. I would like to one day ski somehere other than the east coast of the US.
